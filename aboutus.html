ABOUT US

Creating a new concept at Antique Fractions

High-grade antiques perform extremely well amongst alternative asset classes. Traditionally, this sector has been the exclusive domain of the super wealthy and beyond the reach of everyday people like you and me. Our vision is to ‘democratise’ it, thus offering everybody the chance to collect and benefit from extraordinary returns.

Antique Fractions is an exciting new venture which has been set up by the team from Rosewin Holdings, part of the Fah Mai Holdings group. The idea of Antique Fractions is to give people the opportunity to own and trade fractions of antiques in an affordable way.

“For as long as I can remember, antiques have been a passion of mine. Now, my team and I get a huge amount of pleasure from finding and acquiring beautiful historical items from around the world, and we want our members to feel that same enjoyment as they build their portfolios!” - Daniel Monk, Managing Director.

Antique Fractions’ mission is to acquire, manage, and trade a wide range of items, ensuring the best possible gains for the company and our clients. Antique Fractions will ultimately become the primary channel through which the Rosewin Holdings Antique Collection is offloaded in years to come.

For as little as £50, you can become an antique owner today.
